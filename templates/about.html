Meet the team behind SNIC 2026

Our Mission

As a team, we are launching a 5-week Investment Championship designed to strengthen critical thinking, improve financial literacy, and encourage collaborative learning, all while having fun in a competitive, engaging environment.

What We Do

SNIC organizes annual investment simulation championships where participants manage virtual portfolios and compete using real-time market data.

The platform replicates real-world financial markets, offering a realistic, risk-free trading experience.

The championship provides hands-on exposure to stocks, bonds, ETFs, and other asset classes.
