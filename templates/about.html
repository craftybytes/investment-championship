The Committee

Our Mission

The Swiss National Investment Championship (SNIC for short) is dedicated to fostering financial literacy and investment acumen among students and young professionals across Switzerland. We believe that understanding financial markets and investment strategies is crucial for building a secure financial future, and we're committed to providing a platform where participants can develop these essential skills in a risk-free, competitive environment.

What We Do

SNIC organizes annual investment simulation competitions that challenge participants to manage virtual portfolios, make strategic investment decisions, and compete against their peers using real-time market data. Our platform provides an authentic trading experience that mirrors real-world financial markets, allowing participants to test their knowledge, refine their strategies, and learn from both successes and failures without risking actual capital.

Through our championship, participants gain hands-on experience with various asset classes including stocks, bonds, ETFs, and other financial instruments. They learn to analyze market trends, assess risk, diversify portfolios, and make informed decisions under pressureâ€”all skills that are invaluable in today's complex financial landscape.

Our Vision

We envision a Switzerland where every individual has the knowledge and confidence to make informed financial decisions. By engaging students and young professionals in practical investment education, we aim to create a generation of financially savvy citizens who can navigate the complexities of modern financial markets with skill and integrity.

Why It Matters

Financial literacy is more important now than ever before. With increasing complexity in financial products, volatile markets, and the growing importance of personal financial planning, understanding investment principles has become essential. SNIC bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, giving participants the opportunity to learn by doing in a supportive and competitive environment.

Our championship also serves as a networking platform, connecting like-minded individuals who share an interest in finance and investing. Participants have the opportunity to engage with industry professionals, learn from experienced mentors, and build relationships that can benefit their future careers in finance, business, and beyond.

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of fairness, transparency, and educational value in all our competitions. Our rules are designed to ensure a level playing field where success is determined by knowledge, skill, and strategic thinking rather than unfair advantages. We continuously update our platform and resources to reflect current market conditions and best practices in investment management.

Beyond the competition itself, we provide educational resources, workshops, and expert insights to help participants deepen their understanding of financial markets and investment strategies. We believe that every participant, regardless of their final ranking, should come away from SNIC with valuable knowledge and skills that will serve them throughout their lives.

Join Us

Whether you're a student taking your first steps into the world of investing or a young professional looking to sharpen your financial skills, SNIC offers an exciting opportunity to challenge yourself, learn from others, and potentially earn recognition for your investment prowess. We welcome participants of all experience levels and backgrounds who share our passion for financial education and investment excellence.
