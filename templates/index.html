Purpose

We are launching this investment competition to democratize access to financial literacy and investment experience across international schools in Switzerland. Too often, opportunities to develop real-world financial skills are limited to those with existing resources or connections. By hosting this competition, we're creating a level playing field where students can learn, compete, and demonstrate their analytical abilities regardless of their background.

Why We're Qualified To Host

Our team brings firsthand experience to this initiative. Having participated in similar investment competitions, we understand both the challenges participants face and the logistical requirements for running a successful event. This insider perspective allows us to:

Anticipate common questions and pain points, Design rules that are fair and engaging, Create a structure that balances education with competition, Provide meaningful mentorship throughout the process
