Investment Championship Timeline

International School of Lausanne Investment Competition 2026

February 23 – April 2 (5 weeks)

1
Registration & Preparation
February 9-20, 2026 (2 weeks before launch)
February 9: Competition announcement and registration opens
February 9-15: Marketing campaign across SGIS schools
February 16: Optional informational session (virtual/in-person hybrid for all SGIS schools)
February 20, 11:59 PM: Registration closes
February 22: Teams receive login credentials and competition rules packet
2
Competition Launch
February 23, 2026
9:00 AM: Markets open for trading
9:00 AM: Welcome email with starting portfolio details
All Day: Participants make initial portfolio allocations
5:00 PM: First daily leaderboard update posted
3
Active Trading Period
February 24 – March 21, 2026 (4 weeks)
Week 1 (Feb 24-28): Learning Phase
  • Daily leaderboard updates (5:00 PM CET)
  • Participants adjust to market dynamics and get familiar with software
  • Week 1 welcome meeting (online) answering questions especially about documentation and make clear that portfolio value does not equal an instant win
  • Office hours available for technical questions via email
Week 2 (Mar 3-7): Strategy Development
  • March 5: Mid-competition check-in email
  • Participants refine their investment theses
  • First signs of leading strategies emerge
Week 3 (Mar 10-14): Midterm Review
  • March 12, 11:59 PM: Optional midterm report submission (bonus points for analysis quality)
  • March 13: Midterm leaderboard spotlight featuring top performers
  • Strategy adjustments based on performance
Week 4 (Mar 17-21): Final Push
  • March 19: Reminder email about final report requirements
  • Participants make final strategic decisions
  • Increased market monitoring as competition end approaches
4
Competition Close & Final Submissions
March 22-28, 2026
March 22, 4:00 PM: Trading closes (before market close)
March 22-25: Participants prepare final reports
March 25, 11:59 PM: Final investment report due (mandatory)
  • Portfolio performance analysis
  • Investment rationale and strategy
  • Key learnings and reflection
  • 3-5 pages recommended
March 26-28: Judging period (organizers review reports)
5
Results & Recognition
March 29 – April 2, 2026
March 29: Semifinalists announced (Top 3-5 teams)
March 30-31: Optional semifinalist interviews (15 min each, virtual)
April 1: In person final presentations
  • Winners announced via email and school-wide announcement
  • Awards ceremony (4:00 PM CET)
  • Recognition certificates distributed
  • Highlight reel of winning strategies shared
